<h1>TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS: Node 03 (The Body)</h1>

<p><b>Industrial Classification:</b> LITH-BODY-SPEC-03</p>
<p><b>Status:</b> VERIFIED</p>
<p><b>Target Matrix:</b> High-Tensile Lignocellulosic Substrate</p>
<p><b>Deployment Tiers:</b> Tier I (Industrial/Lab) / Tier II (In-Field)</p>

<h2>1. FIBER PREPARATION (BIO-SOFTENING)</h2>

<h3>1.1 Biological Ramp</h3>
<p>Feedstock: Raw palm fiber, hemp, or flax (decorticated).</p>
<p>Pre-Treatment: 0.5% Calcium Carbonate soak for 12 hours.</p>
<p>Inoculation: Aspergillus oryzae at 2g spores per 1kg dry fiber.</p>
<p>Incubation: 32°C (90°F), 90% RH for 72 hours.</p>
<p>Verification: Fiber separates by hand without snapping.</p>

<h3>1.2 Milling & Freeness</h3>
<p>Tier I: Hollander Beater. Target: 450 CSF.</p>
<p>Tier II: Manual mallet or mortar/pestle until gelatinous slurry forms.</p>

<h2>2. CHITOSAN BRIDGE (SIZING)</h2>

<p>Function: Prevents pigment sinking and oil haloing.</p>

<p>Composition:</p>
<p>Distilled Water: 98%</p>
<p>Chitosan (High MW): 1%</p>
<p>Acetic Acid (5%): 1%</p>

<p>Application: Immersion or brush coating. Air-dry at 20°C.</p>
<p>Result: Insoluble crystalline fiber coating within substrate pores.</p>

<h2>3. PHYSICAL ATTRIBUTES</h2>

<p>Light Grade: 90–120 gsm</p>
<p>Heavy Grade: 250–350 gsm</p>
<p>Surface pH: 6.5–7.5</p>
<p>Dimensional Stability: <2% shrinkage under tension drying</p>

<h2>4. NODE INTEGRATION LOGIC</h2>

<p>Node 01: Pressed finish required for chromatic retention.</p>
<p>Node 02: Tooth finish required for mechanical impasto anchoring.</p>
