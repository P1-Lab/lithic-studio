<h1>BUSINESS CASE: Node 03 (The Body)</h1>

<p><b>Industrial Classification:</b> LITH-BIZ-03</p>
<p><b>Target Sector:</b> Eco-Luxury Hospitality, Regional Tourism, Boutique Stationery, Heritage Branding</p>
<p><b>Operation Scale:</b> 1–3 Person Micro-Manufactory</p>

<h2>1. VALUE PROPOSITION: “PLACE AS PAPER”</h2>

<p>Node 03 functions as a direct conversion system from regional biomass into cultural substrate.</p>

<p>Hospitality Application: Hotels and lodges present notepaper made from local agricultural waste, turning lodging into material provenance.</p>

<p>Restaurant Application: Menus printed on substrate derived from harvest byproducts (husks, stalks, trimmings), reinforcing closed-loop food identity.</p>
