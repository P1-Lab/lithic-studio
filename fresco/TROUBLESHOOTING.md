  <p><strong>TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE: Node 02 (The Form)</strong></p>

  <p><strong>Industrial Classification:</strong> LITH-FORM-TS-02<br>
  <strong>Status:</strong> VERIFIED<br>
  <strong>Scope:</strong> Mechanical Stability, Curing Failures, and Matrix Integrity</p>

  <p><strong>1. MECHANICAL & THIXOTROPIC FAILURES</strong></p>

  <p><strong>Peak Collapse (Slump)</strong><br>
  Symptom: loss of vertical relief after application.<br>
  Cause: oil-to-fiber imbalance or moisture absorption in fibers.<br>
  Fix: verify dry Palm Fiber / Citrus Pomace ratios.<br>
  Recovery: add 0.5% increments of fumed silica or kaolin until peak stability returns.</p>

  <p><strong>Gritty or Poor Flow</strong><br>
  Symptom: abrasive, breaking texture under tool.<br>
  Cause: oversized citrus grit or weak Nori dispersion.<br>
  Fix: rescreen pomace; fully hydrate Nori matrix.<br>
  Recovery: add 2% refined walnut oil and re-fold.</p>

  <p><strong>2. CURING & CHEMICAL FAILURES</strong></p>

  <p><strong>Skinning in Storage</strong><br>
  Symptom: hardened layer on surface of container.<br>
  Cause: oxygen exposure in headspace.<br>
  Fix: eliminate air exposure via full-fill containers.<br>
  Recovery: remove skin; do not remix.</p>

  <p><strong>Extended Tack</strong><br>
  Symptom: surface remains sticky beyond drying window.<br>
  Cause: poor airflow or incorrect oil selection.<br>
  Fix: ensure refined walnut oil and airflow control.<br>
  Recovery: maintain 25°C forced air (do not exceed 30°C).</p>

  <p><strong>3. INTEGRITY & ADHESION FAILURES</strong></p>

  <p><strong>Haloing</strong><br>
  Symptom: oil migration into substrate.<br>
  Cause: insufficient substrate sizing (Node 03 failure).<br>
  Fix: apply 1.0% chitosan sizing layer.<br>
  Recovery: minor haloing may be overpainted after set.</p>

  <p><strong>Delamination</strong><br>
  Symptom: lifting or cracking from substrate.<br>
  Cause: poor mechanical anchoring or substrate mismatch.<br>
  Fix: ensure proper palm fiber length (0.5–2cm).<br>
  Recovery: inject chitosan or rabbit skin glue behind failure zone.</p>

  <p><strong>4. BIOLOGICAL FAILURES</strong></p>

  <p><strong>Rancid Odor</strong><br>
  Symptom: sour or decomposing smell.<br>
  Cause: residual moisture leading to oil hydrolysis.<br>
  Fix: enforce strict dehydration of all botanicals.<br>
  Recovery: TERMINATE BATCH.</p>

  <p><strong>A=A.</strong> Precision in compounding prevents failure in cure.</p>
